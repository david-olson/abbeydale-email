Abbeydale Studio: Charlotte opens late March 2017

Why are we moving? The shortest and most honest answer is for you. Still uptown and just a stones throw from the old spot we’ll be located in the historic Tryon Plaza building located at the corner of Trade and Tryon...our city's center.

The new location is by appointment only. Our decision to move off the street was very strategic as we've always been more focused on the customer experience rather than any “walk in traffic” that might occur. The move to the new location will provide a more intimate setting and ultimately a better experience for you, our priority. We like to dive deep and really get to know our customers, their needs, their nuance and we feel they deserve our 100% undivided attention. Your personal brand is incredibly important and so is your experience with us. Welcome to the new ABBEYDALE.
